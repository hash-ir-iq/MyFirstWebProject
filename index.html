10/25/25: Paris Adventure

Today I visited the Eiffel Tower for the first time. The view from the top was absolutely breathtaking! I treated myself to a warm croissant and enjoyed people-watching in the park.

10/26/25: Venice Canals

Venice is even more magical than I imagined. The canals shimmered in the evening sun, and I took a gondola ride under the famous Rialto Bridge. Can’t wait to visit again!
